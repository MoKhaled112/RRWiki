About the bot:

Automation:

Rumble Royale is a text-based Battle Royale Discord bot; every player will be put into a game where they fight against each other until there is only one winner. The last man standing will be awarded gold for his victory.

With the gold, players can purchase backpacks using the ?shop command. The backpacks can then be opened using the ?open command.

Getting started:

Invited the bot to your server, but not sure what to do next? You can use ?help command for a list of all commands and some other info about the bot. You can also read the FAQs section on our website or browse the information in the channels of the Rumble Royale server.
